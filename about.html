About Me

Designer, artist, & cowboy.

Nice to meet you! I’m Gina, my pronouns are she/her, and I am a product designer with a passion for visual design and empowering users. I grew up in Sugar Land, TX (yes, Sugar Land), and currently live in New York City, where I'm designing cool things at Pearpop.

My design process is influenced by my background in fine arts and my desire to explore complex societal systems. I graduated from New York University with a B.S. in Integrated Digital Media and a minor in Studio Art, where I incorporated my multidisciplinary art, engineering, media, and technology studies into my craft, turning me into the designer I am today.

In my personal life, I have a reputation for collecting street sightings of Papyrus (the typeface), wearing graphic eyeliner, citing Wikipedia, playing Sudoku on the subway, and being an avid AMC Stubs A-Lister.
