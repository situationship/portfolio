Overview

While socially distancing at home, I tried my hand at the Daily UI design challenge. I've seen a fair share of designers trying out this challenge, as well as criticizing it, but I thought it would be fun to take this opportunity to 1) go back to my roots of visual design and 2) keep my creative gears turning and 3) give myself some busy work while staying indoors all day! Some of my favorite designs from the challenge are posted here, but if you're interested in viewing all 100 days, they are all available on my Dribbble.

Conclusion

This challenge allowed me to not only push my creative boundaries with design, but also was a great way to add to my daily routine and help me stay organized and manage my time during a quarantine. I had a lot of fun doing this, and would recommend other designers to try it out! Once again, you can view all of my designs for this challenge on Dribbble.
